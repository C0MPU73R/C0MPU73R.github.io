The Way Life Will Advance

The concept of artificial intelligence has been around since the early 1940s and 1950s, pioneered by computer scientists and mathematicians like Alan Turing and John McCarthy. The integration of AI in consumer electronics is a recent trend in the last 2 decades or so, and has pushed humanity to further feats. Although there is concern over the risks of AI, I will explain that the productive benefits of current AI technology outweigh the current risks.

Smart devices integrated with virtual intelligent systems

Based on the efforts an innovations at several tech companies such as Microsoft, Apple, and Google, AI technology integrated into consumer electronics has manifested into the new normal. With it, we are able to simultaneous check email, messages, GPS, and calls. All of which we are able to control with less effort than before, through the use of the digital assistant.
